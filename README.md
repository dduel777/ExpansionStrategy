## Growth strategy summary (CAStrategy doc)

**Phase 1 — Fill current capacity before adding overhead** (Now → Month 6)
Run a provider-level capacity audit in Valant. Optimize utilization of existing part-time providers before hiring. Close Humana MA gap. Audit patient retention and no-show rates. Implement GoHighLevel reminder workflows.

**Phase 2 — Build the local footprint** (Months 3–12)
Execute sublease nodes in OC, San Diego, and Inland Empire. Each node unlocks a GBP listing and a local PCP referral base. Payer contracts are already statewide — these nodes are marketing infrastructure, not credentialing vehicles. NAP consistency across GBP, NPPES, and payer directories is critical.

**Phase 3 — Hire into proven markets** (Months 9–18)
Hire after sublease nodes demonstrate patient demand. Target providers leaving Mindpath, LifeStance, and Geode. Recruiting pitch: no productivity quotas, VA-handled admin, panel sized to hours worked. Use capacity formula (clinical hrs × recall interval) rather than fixed panel cap.

**Phase 4 — Acquisition window** (Months 18–36)
Target solo CA psychiatrists and small groups (2–5 providers) looking to exit. Acquire patient panels and payer contracts; fold providers into existing infrastructure. One acquisition at a time, minimum 6 months between transactions. Requires healthcare M&A counsel review.

## Quality floor (non-negotiable)

- **Intakes:** 60 minutes — no compression under volume pressure
- **Follow-ups:** 30 min standard · 20 min for experienced providers at appropriate acuity only (defined triage criteria in Valant)
- **Panel size:** Capacity-based formula, not a fixed cap. Monitor recall interval drift in Valant as the overload signal
- **Provider continuity:** No reassignment without patient knowledge and consent
- **Billing:** Insurance verified 48 hrs pre-appointment · copay confirmed before visit · zero claims >30 days unsubmitted · weekly audit
- **Communication:** Portal messages ≤24 hr response · billing disputes acknowledged ≤48 hrs, resolved ≤10 business days
